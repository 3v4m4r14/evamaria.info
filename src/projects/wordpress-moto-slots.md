Moto Slots is a playful slot machine game, inspired by [my motorcycling group](https://evamaria.info/kuidas-ma-poldu-kundsin-ja-motogrupi-lahku-loin/)’s shared love for motorcycles, coffee, and hedgehogs. Built using Cursor AI, this project was a hands-on exercise to explore and learn vibe coding, combining creative theming with interactive features.

**Try it here:** [https://3v4m4r14.github.io/motoslots/](https://3v4m4r14.github.io/motoslots/)

## Key Features

- **Jackpot Wins:** Exciting jackpot mechanics to enhance gameplay.

- **Responsive Layout:** Optimized for smooth play across devices.

- **Immersive Audio:**
    - Motorcycle engine sounds as background ambience
    
    - Revving effects for spins
    
    - Distinct audio cues for regular and jackpot wins
    
    - Audio toggle for player preference

- **Dynamic Win Animations:** Visually engaging feedback for every win.

- **Rules & Payouts Popup:** Quick access to game mechanics and payout information.

- **Adjustable Bet Amount:** Players can modify their bet to suit their play style.

- **Emojis as Reel Icons:** Fun, themed symbols featuring motorcycles, coffee, and hedgehogs.

## Technical Highlights

- **Return to Player (RTP):**
    - Used Cursor AI to run simulations and estimate game balance.
    
    - Current estimated RTP: 90.37% (subject to further tuning).

## My Role

- Conceived and developed the project as a personal learning initiative.

- Explored vibe coding with Cursor AI, from concept to implementation.

- Focused on feature experimentation and creative design, rather than formal user research.
