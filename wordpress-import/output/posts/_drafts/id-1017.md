They say [dogs look like their owners](https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/abs/10.1111/j.0956-7976.2004.00684.x) and if somebody looked at us, they would be forced to agree. To begin with, we are both fair-haired. Just as I have long hair, you have a mane around your neck.
